About me

A quick idea of who I am.

I am a 23 year old Canadian Developer who's been working on platform since roughly 2011, though my history with the platform goes back further than that to when I first started playing in 2008! While I've only been professionally working for a few years now, my experience with the platform stems back more than 10 years as a hobbyist.

I've worked on a wide range of projects, from small experiments to large scale games with millions of visits. I'm very well versed in a large set of skills and can easily prototype as well as work full-stack to deliver a game from start to finish. I'm also very passionate about the platform and love to help others learn and grow as developers.
